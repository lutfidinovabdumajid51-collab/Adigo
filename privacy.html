Privacy Policy for Adigo Taxi

Last updated: January 25, 2026

Adigo Taxi is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how your information is collected, used, and disclosed by Adigo Taxi & Pochta.

1. Information We Collect

  • Authentication: We use Firebase Anonymous Authentication to collect a unique identifier (UID).
  • Order Details: Pickup/drop-off locations, phone numbers, and price offers.
  • Device Data: Device model, OS version, and unique device identifiers (DeviceId).

2. Data Usage

We use this data to provide taxi/delivery services, ensure app security, prevent fraud, and allow communication between drivers and users.

3. Data Deletion

Users have the right to cancel or delete their orders within the app.
